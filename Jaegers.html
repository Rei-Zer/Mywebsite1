Jaegers

    One of the best jaegers ever made.A mark 6 Jaeger.



  • Titan Redeemer

    It has a huge spike ball which is very effective in close combat.A mark 6 jaeger.



  • Saber Athena

    The faster jaeger ever made.A mark 7



  • Guardian Bravo

    An ark whip which causes heavy damage. A mark 6.



  • Bracer Phoenix

    It has a huge cannon at its waist allowing it to shoot at both infront and behind. A spectacular mark 5 who can run with mark 6.



  • November Ajax

    It has electric cables that comes out from its knuckles and acts as a police force for the PPDC as people try to create their own Jaegers by stealing parts from destroyed ones. Mark 6.



  • Obsidian Fury

    A jaeger which was infected by kaiju and became something called a rogue Kaiju-Jaeger hybrid and attackes the PPDC. It destroyed by Gypsy Danger in the north pole.



  • Gypsy Avenger. My favourite Jaeger and is a improved version of Gypsy Danger in the first movie. It was made to pay respect to the fallen Gypsy Danger as it was blown up to close the breach. Gypsy Avenger is more than just a Jaeger, she is a symbol of hope to millions.



  • Scrapper

    This was a very small home-made Jaeger by Amara which was crucial in the final fight with the mega-kaiju.
